## Outcome Goal

- Linked Horizon: "24 Months — It runs the operation" where nowu functions as infrastructure/company OS by practice, and is stable enough to ship as framework, product, or service.
- Desired Change: nowu becomes the default operating substrate for project execution and collaboration between humans and AI, with governance and portability as normal behavior.
- Non-Goals: Lock-in through opaque proprietary data silos; immediate enterprise-scale positioning; implementation-level architecture mandates.

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Operationally significant project decisions and captures routinely flow through nowu as the primary execution system.
- [ ] The system demonstrates stability and portability sufficient for external adoption as framework/product/service.
- [ ] Collaboration flows between humans and AI are first-class, auditable, and reliable across project lifecycle stages.
- [ ] Data governance expectations (inspectability, ownership, controlled sharing) are met as part of default workflows.
- [ ] This goal can be marked `achieved` when nowu is used as operational infrastructure in sustained real-world practice and can be adopted beyond the original builder context.

## Solution Shape

- Form: An operational collaboration layer that coordinates project execution, preserves institutional memory, and exposes portable artifacts for adoption.
- Key Capabilities: Organization-wide workflow routing; multi-actor collaboration protocols; governance-aware knowledge access; productization-ready packaging and onboarding pathways.
- Main Tradeoffs: Broad operational coverage and governance maturity versus maintaining the simplicity and speed valued by solo/small-team origins.
- Sequencing Notes: Reach multi-project compounding and durable handoff first, then harden stability/governance/collaboration for external adoption and operational centrality.
