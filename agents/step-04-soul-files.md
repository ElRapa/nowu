# Step 4 — soul: Identity Files

**This is a HUMAN task. No coding agent needed.**
**Estimated time: 30–60 minutes.**

## What to Do

These files define the rules of your system. You must write them yourself so they reflect YOUR vision and values — not a template.

### Files to finalize

#### `soul/VISION.md`
Answer these questions in plain prose (1–2 paragraphs each):
1. What is `nowu` trying to achieve? Why does it exist?
2. Who is it for? (Just you for now)
3. What does success look like in 6 months? In 2 years?
4. What is NOT the goal? (Scope boundaries)

#### `soul/AGENTS.md`
Write the operating rules for every coding agent that works in this repo.
Include:
- The four roles: Framer, Shaper, Implementer, Reviewer
- The WAL protocol: "Before every response, write important decisions to know"
- The VBR protocol: "Before claiming done, run the tests and verify output"
- The approval tier rules: Tier 1 (auto), Tier 2 (batch), Tier 3 (immediate block)
- What to do when stuck

#### `DECISIONS.md`
Review the pre-filled decisions (D-001 to D-005) in the file.
- Add your own commentary or adjustments if anything needs changing
- Add any decisions YOU made that aren't there yet

### After completing this step

Commit with message: `feat(soul): finalize VISION, AGENTS, and initial decisions`

Then continue to Step 5.
